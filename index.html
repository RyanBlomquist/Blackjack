21 Blackjack

Rules

  • The goal of the game is to beat the house and get as high a score as possible without exceeding 21.
  • Number cards are scored equal to their value and face cards are worth 10 points.
  • Aces are worth 1 or 11 points. They are scored as 11 points until your score exceeds 21, at which point they will be scored as 1.
  • Click the center deck to take another card.
  • Click 'Done' when you are finished taking cards.
  • After you are done, the house will draw until their score is at least 17.
  • Whoever has the highest score without exceeding 21 wins the match.
  • The house wins in the event of a tie.
  • The house's deck is on top, and yours is below.
